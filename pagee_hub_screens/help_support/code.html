Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect a custom domain? expand_more
Connecting a custom domain is seamless. Navigate to Settings > Domains, and enter your URL. We provide a simplified CNAME record that you can copy-paste into your registrar's DNS settings. SSL is automatically provisioned within 15 minutes.
Can I export my Pagee Studio projects? expand_more
Yes, all Premium plans include full code export. You can download your site as a high-performance static build (HTML/CSS/JS) ready for any hosting provider.
What are the limits on the Premium Plan? expand_more
The Premium Plan is designed for high-growth creators. You get unlimited landing pages, priority support, and up to 100k monthly unique visitors. For larger needs, explore our Enterprise options.
How does the review moderation work? expand_more
Our AI-driven moderation system filters spam and offensive content automatically. You maintain full control to approve, hide, or respond to individual reviews from your dashboard.
